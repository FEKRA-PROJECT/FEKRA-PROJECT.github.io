About Us

We Aim to help you achieve your dreams

FEKRA" is a comprehensive educational project founded by Ibrahim Mohammad and Al-Jalilah in the year 2021. With a strong focus on education, FEKRA aims to provide students with enhanced educational opportunities, including specialized preparation for the SAT. The project offers private lessons specifically tailored to SAT preparation, with the goal of helping students excel in both the math and English sections of the exam. Additionally, FEKRA places emphasis on developing writing skills, fostering analytical thinking, and promoting multilingualism among students. By offering a range of educational resources and services, FEKRA seeks to raise awareness and contribute to the overall educational development of society, while also equipping students with the tools they need to succeed in their SAT exams and beyond.
